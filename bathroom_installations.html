- Bathroom Installations -

Ethos installation services install new bathrooms, shower rooms. Our team carries out all aspects of a completely finished bathroom from the ripping out of an existing suite to all the plumbing, electrics, tiling, flooring and decoration of a finished new roomBook a free estimate: 020 7751 3344

As with all our work, Ethos provides a guarantee on bathroom installations. We guarantee our labour for a minimum of twelve months and supply full manufacturers warrantees on all the products we supply.
