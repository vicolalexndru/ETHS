Planning

From a brand new open plan kitchen and dining room, to extra bedrooms and bathrooms, Simply Extend is here to help you plan your extra space. There are many different types and sizes of extensions to choose from as well as glazing options including velux windows and bi-folding doors. Click here for more ideas on glazing options or visit our gallery for inspiration on planning and designing your own extension. All photos are taken from Simply Extend projects so you will be able to see our level of workmanship and attention to detail. When it comes to planning your extension, our all in one package means that if required, one of our architects will visit your home to complete a survey and produce a set of plans according to your wishes and feasibility of the space available. Simply Extend can also help with structural calculations and dealing with building control as well as submitting your plans under permitted development or full planning.
