Renovations

Is your property drastically different from how you want it? perhaps the interior needs a new layout with new look, or completely modernising? Renovating the property, in part or from the ground up, may be the solution for you. If you are planning to sell spending some money on renovation where you could expect more on sale. We have a full team who get in touch with our external partners Architects, Surveyors, Structural Engineers, Building Inspectors, builders and joiners in order to fully realise your dream home. Maybe you want to change the function of a room, repartition or remove walls to change the layout of existing spaces, or create additional light with new windows Increase Space can help you. We’ve worked on projects big to small, from hiding unwanted features and creating storage spaces to creating entirely new rooms, and moving bathrooms between storeys. To discuss how we can create the best design and solution for you, get in contact for a free survey and quotation.
